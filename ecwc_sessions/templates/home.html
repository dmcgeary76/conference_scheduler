Welcome to the Early Childhood Winter Conference Registration Tool

The purpose of this tool is to help you plan your session attendance before you arrive at the conference. The steps to using this tool are simple:

  1. Click on one of the links at the top of the page to view all of the sessions available for a given timeslot.
  2. To view the details for a given session, just click on the title of the session.
  3. If you like the session description, press the "Register" button to add the session to your schedule.

You can replace an item on your schedule at any time by simply choosing a new session for that timeslot. Please note that while you can certainly attend sessions without using this application, registering for sessions using this tool will guarantee you a seat for the session and provide an opporunity for you to receive future correspondence about the session topic.

If you experience any issues related to the use of this tool, please contact the administrator.
